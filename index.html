About NexaOrb

NexaOrb Consulting Group provides centralized delivery of predefined professional services that drive product adoption, predictable outcomes, and stronger margins at scale. Anchored by cross-functional practices, we embed automation and engineering excellence into every stage of the customer lifecycle.

NexaOrb Consulting

NexaOrb Center of Engineering (CoE)

Engineering services for integration, automation, DevOps, and extensibility across multi-cloud platforms, third-party ecosystems, and mission-critical workloads within joint delivery programs.

Technology-driven migration services, specialized toolchains, and expert squads that re-host workloads (P2V, V2V, V2C, C2C) across leading private, sovereign, and hyperscale cloud platforms.
